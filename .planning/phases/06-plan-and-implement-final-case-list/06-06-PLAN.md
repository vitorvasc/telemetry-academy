```python
from opentelemetry.sdk.trace.sampling import (
    TraceIdRatioBased,
    ParentBased,
    ALWAYS_ON,
    ALWAYS_OFF,
)
from opentelemetry.sdk.trace import TracerProvider

# Sample 10% of traces
sampler = TraceIdRatioBased(0.1)

# Parent-based: defer to parent's sampling decision; if no parent, use ratio
sampler = ParentBased(TraceIdRatioBased(0.5))

# ALWAYS_ON: capture every trace (development/debugging)
sampler = ALWAYS_ON

provider = TracerProvider(sampler=sampler)
```

Phase 1 description (concept-first, Markdown):
1. "What is Sampling?" — 2-3 sentences. Sampling determines which traces are recorded and which are dropped. At high traffic (100k req/s), storing every trace is cost-prohibitive. Sampling lets you keep a representative subset while controlling storage costs.
2. "Head-Based Sampling" — explain: the sampling decision is made at the start of the trace (the "head"), before any spans are created. `TraceIdRatioBased(0.1)` samples 10% of traces based on the trace ID hash — deterministic, no coordination needed.
3. "ParentBased vs TraceIdRatioBased" — key distinction: `TraceIdRatioBased` makes a fresh sampling decision per root span. `ParentBased` respects the parent's sampling decision — if the upstream sampled the trace, all downstream spans are also sampled. Use `ParentBased` in production to keep traces intact.
4. "The Danger of Over-Sampling" — 1-2 sentences: "Setting sampling to 1% means 99% of errors are dropped before reaching your backend. A 5% error rate looks like 0.05% — effectively invisible."
5. "The Situation:" — the team deployed a new sampler with ratio=0.01 (1%) to cut costs. An error spike began 2 hours later, but no alerts fired because 99% of error traces were dropped.
6. "Your Mission:" — Configure a `TracerProvider` with `ALWAYS_ON` sampler to ensure all 5 test spans are captured. Then switch to `ParentBased(TraceIdRatioBased(0.5))` and observe approximately half are captured. Add a span attribute `sampler.configured: 'always_on'` to confirm your sampler config.
7. "Key Concepts:" — TraceIdRatioBased, ParentBased, ALWAYS_ON, ALWAYS_OFF, sampling head decision.
